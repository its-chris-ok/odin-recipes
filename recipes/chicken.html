Baked Chicken

~Crispy baked chicken thighs~

Description

This easy-to-make baked chicken recipe is perfect for late workdays or meal prep. With a few simple ingredients that you probably already have on-hand, will certainly be a mainstay!

Ingredients

Main Ingredients

  • 6x Chicken Thighs (skin-on, bone-in)

Seasonings

  • Salt & Pepper (to taste)
  • 1 pinch garlic powder
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 475F. Set a wire rack inside a broiler pan or baking pan.
  2. Pat chicken thighs dry with paper towels. Lift skin up; sprinkle salt, pepper, garlic powder, and cayenne pepper under the skin. Replace skin and season exterior of chicken thigh to taste.
  3. Arrange on the rack.
  4. Roast in the preheated oven until golden, about 20 minutes. Reduce oven temperature to 400F. Continue roasting until juices run clear, about 30 mins or more. Let rest for 5 minutes before serving.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy!
